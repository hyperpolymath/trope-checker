== What this is

Theorem B is the deferred metatheory of the trope-particularity *calculus* (spec
`spec/calculus.adoc`, `:revnumber: 0.1`) over the grade algebra. It is developed
*intrinsically* in Agda 2.8.0 + agda-stdlib, `--safe --without-K`, in
`verification/proofs/agda/theorem-b/`, REUSING the echo-types graded-reindexing
*style* (`echo-types/proofs/agda/EchoGraded.agda` — the thin-poset reindexing
modality; `EchoIndexed.agda` — indexed functoriality; these are the
"`EchoVariance`" graded muscle the task names).

It sits ON TOP of the *FROZEN INTERFACE* — the grade algebra established in Lean 4
(`verification/proofs/lean4/grade-boundary/GradeBoundary.lean`, sorry-free, axioms
`propext`/`Quot.sound` only). The four established facts are re-established in Agda
here as the foundation (`TB.Grade`), then the calculus metatheory is built over them.

[IMPORTANT]
====
*Build.* `agda All.agda` and `agda Smoke.agda` both exit 0 under `--safe
--without-K`. A cold rebuild (`agda --ignore-interfaces All.agda`) exits 0.
*Postulates: ZERO* (see §"Postulate ledger"). No `sorry`, no `TERMINATING`, no
`trustMe`. The one genuinely-open obligation (subject reduction through `fix`) is
*NOT-REACHED and NOT postulated* — it is documented as a precise obstruction.
====

== Per-target verdict

=== Target 1 — encode the grade as a NON-COMMUTATIVE graded structure (intrinsic) — *PROVED*

`TB.Grade` ports the carrier (Δ, Fate, Bond, Merge, Grade) and ▷ clause-for-clause
from the Lean, with first-argument-match so F2 holds *definitionally*
(`dropped ▷f falsified = dropped ≠ falsified = falsified ▷f dropped`). F1/F2/F3 and
the F4 absorbing lemmas are re-established. `TB.Syntax` is the *intrinsic* calculus
`Γ ⊢ A ! g` (a derivation is a well-typed-and-well-graded term): variables carry ε;
the unary effect formers POST-compose their atom on the right (`g ▷ atom`); the
binary seams `app`/`let` put the earlier effect on the LEFT (`gx ▷ gf ▷ g`,
`g₁ ▷ g₂`, CBV argument-first); T-Sub is a leaf, one-sided along `_⊑_`. F2 is
*mandatory and respected*: ▷ is a bare non-commutative function — no
`CommMonoid`/Special-Frobenius instance anywhere.

NOTE (honest scope): the field-presence indexing of `Trope[S]`/`FloatingQuality`
(the S ⊆ Φ subtyping) is modelled coarsely (a single `⋆Trope` plus the
detach/collapse type-changes); the *grade* discipline is exact. The binary `fuse`
former (needing the retention-join ⊔) is omitted from the intrinsic core; its grade
rule `(g₁ ⊔ g₂) ▷ label` is recorded but not built (orthogonal lattice machinery,
no bearing on F2/F4). These are deliberate scoping choices, stated loudly, not
silent weakenings.

=== Target 2 — substitution-admissibility / reindexing ("tropes follow values through cut") — *PROVED + sharpness refutation*

The substitution lemma `subst : Subst Γ Δ → Γ ⊢ A ! g → Δ ⊢ A ! g` preserves the
grade `g` *EXACTLY* — the conclusion's grade is the same `g`, no ▷ reorder anywhere.
This is the machine-checked form of "tropes follow values through cut". It is
insensitive to F2 precisely because a value (and a variable) carries ε, the
two-sided unit of ▷; single substitution `_[_]` inherits exactness.

The F2 boundary is itself a *theorem*, not a gap: `cut-left-grade` pins the oriented
cut grade `g₁ ▷ g₂`; `cut-orientation-matters` exhibits computations whose forward
composition differs from the swapped one (witness `dropped`/`falsified`); and
`cut-symmetric-fails` refutes any orientation-free computational-cut law. So the
value lemma is *sharp* — F2 forbids its symmetric generalisation. This is exactly
the predicted F2 wall, delivered as a refutation rather than an unproven obligation.

=== Target 3 — subject reduction respecting the three-tier F4 boundary — *PARTIAL (β/let core PROVED; `fix` NOT-REACHED)*

Reduction `_↝_` is indexed at a *fixed* `(Γ, A, g)`, so subject reduction — type AND
grade preserved by a step — holds BY CONSTRUCTION: it is the well-typedness of
`_↝_`'s constructors. The substantive content is that the β and let-value steps
*can* be typed at one grade index, which is the grade-COLLAPSE equations
`β-redex-grade : (ε ▷ ε) ▷ g ≡ g` and `let-redex-grade : ε ▷ g ≡ g` — genuine F1
unit-law equations (LHS syntactically distinct from RHS, *not* the tautology
`g ≡ g`), the contractum having grade `g` by Target 2. Hence the grade — and so the
*tier* — is preserved *EXACTLY*: `β-tier-preserved`/`let-tier-preserved`
(`tierOf ((ε ▷ ε) ▷ g) ≡ tierOf g`, non-vacuous), the strongest form of respecting
the boundary — no tier moves across a step.

NOTE: the `reduct` projection (which returns the well-graded contractum) carries no
directional content on its own — all the force is in `_↝_`'s indexing and the
collapse equations above. It is named honestly, not as a free-standing theorem.

The boundary under genuine grade *composition* (the effect formers, and any
effect-sequencing reduction) is respected in the CLOSURE sense (`TB.Tier`):
`deceptive-absorbs-▷` (L5 irreversibility, made dynamic — applying any further
effect to a deceptive computation keeps it deceptive: `attenuate/project-stays-
deceptive`), `core-closed-▷`/`Q-cancelʳ` (the cancellative core stays in the core
and cancels), and the six `*-not-cancel` witnesses (three-tier strictness).

[IMPORTANT]
.FINDING (F2×F4): tier-preservation is CLOSURE, not a homomorphism
====
The naive single-tier meet-homomorphism `tierOf (g ▷ h) ≡ tierOf g ⊓ tierOf h` is
*FALSE* on the fate coordinate — `fate-tier-meet-hom-fails`: `dropped ▷f falsified
= dropped` (honest), but `fateTier dropped ⊓ fateTier falsified = deceptive`. The
honest left-absorbing head `dropped` *shields* a later deceptive `falsified`. This
is the same non-commutativity phenomenon (F2) as the L4-monotonicity failure
(`fate-L4-fails`, ported from `Soundness.idr`). It is *why* tier-preservation had to
be stated as set-membership closure and *why* the ⊑/L4 route is unsound — both are
kept as compiled guard theorems so no later work re-introduces the wrong route.
====

NOT-REACHED — subject reduction through `fix`/lfp (calculus §7). Honestly
characterised in `TB.Reduction` (and *not postulated*): two things are genuinely
missing — (1) an operational model (no ↝ rule for `fix`, no observed-retention ρ;
the spec is a type discipline), and (2) the O4 limit carrier for ordering iterated
attenuation beyond ω (Conat-vs-tag). The lfp's fidelity endpoint lands in the
▷-fixed absorbing tops `{total, unknown}`, so the *grade* is tier-stable; the
unproven part is the operational unfold-preserves-tier step. This obstruction is
the missing operational semantics + O4, NOT a defect of the grade algebra.

=== Target 4 — Honest Generation (HC-*) and Veridicality (L*) — *PROVED* (reached because 1–3 landed)

`TB.Honest` ports Veridicality (`isDeceptive{Fate,Bond,Merge}`, the uniqueness
lemmas, `deceptiveDuals = (falsified, misbound, conflated)`) and Generation
(`Move`, `moveFate/Bond/Merge`, `movesHonest*`, `genFate/genBond/genMerge`) from the
Idris ground truth. The new content is the *calculus metatheorem*: honesty is closed
under the non-commutative ▷ (`honest-closed-▷`, via per-coordinate
`fate/bond/merge-honest`), and every writable effect atom is honest
(`ε/atten/drop/sever/predicate-atom-honest`). Therefore a term built from the
writable effects can NEVER produce a deceptive grade
(`preserve/attenuate/detach/collapse-stays-honest`) — HC-3 / the prevent-variant,
realised dynamically: the deceptive states are unreachable by construction.

== F2 and F4 respected, not weakened — checklist

* F2 (non-commutativity) is *mandatory* and live: `grade-not-comm` is a pinned
  theorem; ▷ is a bare non-commutative function; no commutative bundle is
  instantiated for `Grade`/`Fate`; the substitution refutation
  (`cut-symmetric-fails`) and the tier finding (`fate-tier-meet-hom-fails`) both
  DEPEND on F2 and would be false under a commutative model.
* F4 (three-tier boundary) is preserved as a *structure* subject reduction respects:
  the cancellative core is closed and cancels; the deceptive tier is forward-closed
  (L5); the honest-but-lossy middle is shown non-cancellative by witnesses; and the
  one place a clean homomorphism would over-claim is exhibited as a refuted finding.
  No statement was silently restricted to make it go through; every scoping choice
  (presence-coarseness, `fuse` omission, `fix` NOT-REACHED) is stated explicitly.
