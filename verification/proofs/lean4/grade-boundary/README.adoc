== Step 0 — provenance (invent nothing)

* Spec is `:revnumber: 0.1`. There is **no v0.2**. v0.1 "O1" (completeness of the
  seven effects) is **out of scope** and untouched.
* `grep -niE 'frobenius|cancellat|conical|coordinate.fact' spec/calculus.adoc` →
  the only hit is **O3** (the produce/demand *adjunction* open question), which is
  unrelated. So **no Frobenius / cancellative / conical framing exists in the
  spec**. This harness is **new work**.
* **CF-as-universal is refuted by L5.** Every composition is a meet with an
  absorbing *deceptive bottom*, so the carrier is **non-cancellative by design**:
  deception must be irreversible (cancellation would let a lie be subtracted back
  out, i.e. forged away). The deliverable is therefore not "prove CF" but to
  characterise the boundary — a sharper, true theorem.

=== Carrier confirmed against the Idris2 source

* `Fidelity.idr`: `Delta = Q ℕ | Total | Unknown`, tropical add, `Unknown` absorbs
  `Total` absorbs finite, unit `Q 0`. Structurally `WithTop (WithTop ℕ)`
  (`Q n ↔ ↑↑n`, `Total ↔ ↑⊤`, `Unknown ↔ ⊤`, `dplus = (+)`, `Q 0 = 0`).
* `Coords.idr`: `Fate` / `Bond` / `Merge` with componentwise `▷` and `⊑`; each
  coordinate has *exactly one* deceptive inhabitant = its unique order-bottom.
* `Grade.idr`: flat record `Fate⁴ × Bond × Merge`, fully independent fields.

=== Discrepancy with the task brief's partition (resolved in favour of the SOURCE)

The brief listed deceptive = `{Dropped, Falsified, Misbound, Conflated, Unknown,
Total}`. The **carrier** says deceptive = the unique absorbing order-bottom per
coordinate only: `Falsified` (fate), `Misbound` (bond), `Conflated` (merge), with
`Unknown` the fidelity bottom. Two reclassifications follow from the source:

* **`Dropped` is honest**, not deceptive — "withheld entirely" (an honest
  omission). Its *deceptive dual* is `Falsified`. In `fateLte` it sits strictly
  above the `Falsified` bottom and is incomparable to `Predicated`.
* **`Total` is honest** — the "∞ total loss" (`Fidelity.idr` l.4); absorbing over
  finite δ but not a lie. `Unknown` (loss of *unknown* amount) is the deceptive
  bottom.

This discrepancy is not a footnote: it is *precisely why* "cancellation fails ⇔
deceptive" is **false**. `Dropped`, `Total`, `severed`, `predicated` are
non-deceptive yet non-cancellative.

== Deliverables (all green, all sorry-free)

=== D1 — Discriminator: DEGENERATE-TO-PRODUCT — *confirmed*, with a refinement

`instance instMonoid : Monoid Grade` is the componentwise product of the coordinate
monoids and type-checks with all laws discharged from the per-coordinate laws — the
verdict is reconfirmed constructively.

**Refinement discovered against the carrier:** the product is **non-commutative**
(`grade_mul_not_comm`). `Fate` has *two* distinct left-absorbing heads — `Dropped`
and `Falsified` — that disagree (`Dropped ▷ Falsified = Dropped` but
`Falsified ▷ Dropped = Falsified`). Hence `CommMonoid`/`AddCommMonoid` **cannot**
model `▷`. This sharpens, but does not overturn, DEGENERATE-TO-PRODUCT: the carrier
is a product *monoid*, just not a commutative one. (Where the brief expected
`AddCommMonoid` to construct, the honest outcome is the captured non-commutativity.)

=== D2 — Conicality (full carrier)

`grade_conical : a * b = 1 → a = 1 ∧ b = 1`. Proven from per-coordinate conicality
(`Fate.conical`, `Bond.conical`, `Merge.conical`). The unit ε is reachable under `▷`
only from ε ▷ ε.

=== D3 — The honest/deceptive cancellation boundary (the target)

* **L5 — deception is irreversible.** Each deceptive bottom is a *left zero*
  (absorbing): `Fate.falsified_absorbing`, `Bond.misbound_absorbing`,
  `Merge.conflated_absorbing`, `Delta.unknown_absorbing`. Hence the full carrier is
  **non-left-cancellative** (`grade_not_leftCancel`), witnessed at a deceptive
  grade. A lie composed with anything stays that same lie — it cannot be cancelled
  back out.
* **The cancellative core.** `Delta.Q` embeds `(ℕ,+,0)` injectively and additively
  (`Delta.Q_add`, `Delta.Q_injective`, `Delta.Q_zero`) and right-cancels on its
  image (`honest_fidelity_cancel`, inherited from `ℕ`). This finite-fidelity region
  is the cancellative submonoid that survives the refutation of CF-as-universal.
* **The boundary is three-tier, not two-tier.** Honest-but-lossy elements are *also*
  non-cancellative — `Bond.severed_honest_not_cancel`,
  `Fate.predicated_honest_not_cancel`, `fidelity_total_honest_not_cancel`. So:
+
[cols="1,3"]
|===
| Tier 1 — cancellative core | finite fidelity `Q ℕ ≅ (ℕ,+,0)`, and the units
| Tier 2 — honest, non-cancellative | `Dropped`, `Predicated`, `Severed`, `Withheld`, `Fused`, `Total` (lossy meets/collapses)
| Tier 3 — deceptive, absorbing bottoms | `Falsified`, `Misbound`, `Conflated`, `Unknown`
|===
+
The non-cancellative set (tiers 2 ∪ 3) strictly contains the deceptive set
(tier 3). L5 = "tier 3 is absorbing ⇒ irreversible". CF-as-universal fails because
tiers 2 ∪ 3 are all non-cancellative — cancellation holds *only* on tier 1.
