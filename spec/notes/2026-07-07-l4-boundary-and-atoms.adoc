= Research note: the L4 boundary, a one-clause diagnosis, and O1 as a theorem
:revdate: 2026-07-07
:toc: macro

[.lead]
Machine-checked analysis of the lax-L4 crack (spec v0.1 §4/§7), a proposed
two-amendment repair, the carrier-relative resolution of O1, and the
consequences for issues #10, #12, #13, #14. Companion Lean developments:
`verification/proofs/lean4/grade-boundary/{TierTwoComplete,L4Monotonicity,EffectAtoms}.lean`
(all sorry-free, axiom-clean). *Nothing in this note changes the shipped
carrier*; the amendments are proposals for owner ratification.

toc::[]

== 1. Where exactly L4 fails

v0.1 §4 states L4 (▷ monotone in each argument w.r.t. ⊑) and §7 leans on it:
"the least fixed point exists because each coordinate lattice is complete and,
by L4, the grade functional is monotone." The Idris2 stream already proved
strict L4 fails on fate (`fateL4MonotonicityFails`). The new Lean development
localises the failure completely. Every counterexample passes through one of
exactly two design points:

1. *The `{Predicated, Dropped}` antichain* (the duck check). Composing a
   collapse after a value that might have been dropped lands on incomparable
   results: `Predicated ▷ Dropped = Dropped` vs
   `Predicated ▷ Atten δ = Predicated`, and `Dropped ∥ Predicated`
   (`Fate.comp_not_monotone_right`, `comp_not_monotone_left'`).
2. *The clause `Dropped ▷ Falsified = Dropped`*. With `Dropped ⊑ Atten δ`,
   composing a downstream falsification gives `Dropped` on one side and
   `Falsified` on the other, and `Dropped ⋢ Falsified`
   (`Fate.comp_not_monotone_left`).

Bond, merge, and the fidelity chain are all monotone (each is meet/tropical
addition on a chain); fate is the only broken coordinate.

== 2. One clause, three pathologies

The clause `Dropped ▷ Falsified = Dropped` is simultaneously:

* *The sole source of non-commutativity.* `GradeBoundary.grade_mul_not_comm`'s
  witness is exactly the pair `(Dropped, Falsified)`; every other pair of fate
  values commutes, and bond/merge/fidelity are commutative. The celebrated
  non-commutativity of the grade monoid is not intrinsic to loss-shape grading
  — it is this clause.
* *The falsify-side L4 failure* (§1, point 2).
* *In tension with L5's moral core.* L5 wants deception infectious and
  irreversible. Under the current clause, an upstream honest *drop* erases a
  downstream *lie*: a stage that fabricates a value for a field it never
  received composes to plain `Dropped`, and the fabrication vanishes from the
  grade. An honest absorber should not launder deception.

== 3. The proposed repair (owner ratification required)

Two amendments, machine-checked in `L4Monotonicity.lean` (`FateA` namespace):

* *(A1)* `Dropped ▷ Falsified = Falsified` — the deceptive bottom becomes a
  **two-sided zero** (`FateA.falsified_two_sided_zero`). L5 strengthens: no
  composition order launders a lie.
* *(A2)* `Dropped ⊑ Predicated` — a checkbox retains at least as much of the
  quality as nothing at all. The duck check survives in the form that matters:
  `Predicated` remains strictly below every `Atten`, so any use that needs the
  actual quack (floor ≥ `Atten`) still rejects both the checkbox and the
  dropped field. What changes is only that a floor of `Dropped` ("I tolerate
  full withholding") now also accepts a checkbox — and in a *retention* order,
  tolerating less while rejecting more was never expressible anyway.

Consequences, all proved:

|===
| Property | Shipped carrier | Amended carrier

| L4 monotonicity (both args) | *fails* (§1) | *holds* (`FateA.comp_mono_left/right`)
| Commutativity | fails (`grade_mul_not_comm`) | *holds* (`FateA.comp_comm`)
| Order shape | lattice with antichain | *chain* (`FateA.le_total`)
| §7 Knaster–Tarski for `fix` | justification cracked | *restored* (complete chain + monotone ▷)
| Deceptive bottoms absorbing (L5) | left only | *two-sided* (strengthened)
| Conicality | holds | holds (`FateA.conical`)
| Three-tier cancellation boundary | holds | holds (`FateA.dropped_still_not_cancel`)
|===

Two knock-ons to be aware of:

* `grade_mul_not_comm` becomes a historical artifact if (A1) is adopted; the
  boundary results survive unchanged in kind.
* The `grade-factorisation` probe's verdict ("any hom to the commutative
  tropical carrier is veridicality-blind") was proved against the shipped
  carrier; under (A1) the grade monoid is itself commutative and the probe's
  premises change — it should be re-run before being cited further.

== 4. O4 / issue #12: the carrier suffices

With (A1)+(A2) the fate coordinate is order-isomorphic to the chain

  Falsified ⊏ Dropped ⊏ Predicated ⊏ Atten(⊤) ⊏ Atten(∞) ⊏ … ⊏ Atten(Q 1) ⊏ Atten(Q 0) ⊏ Present

i.e. three points below a copy of the fidelity chain `ℕ ∪ {∞, ⊤}` (reversed)
below a top. Every coordinate is then a **complete linear order**: arbitrary
infima and suprema exist (any subset of ℕ has a minimum; unbounded subsets meet
∞/⊤). Knaster–Tarski needs nothing more. Transfinite/coinductive carriers would
only be forced by transfinitely deep `fix`-nesting, which finitary syntax
cannot express: any single program's grade is reached within ω iterations of
its (finite) grade functional. Recommendation: *close #12 as "current carrier
suffices, conditional on the §3 repair"* — the gate on #10 lifts.

== 5. The path through CO-1 / issue #10

What #10 still needs is an operational model; the repair removes the blocker
that made its lfp story unsound. Proposed design (for discussion, not yet
mechanized):

1. *Instrumented small-step semantics.* Values are provenance-enriched tropes
   `⟨quality, bearer, context, record⟩`. Each atomic move (`attenuate δ`,
   `predicate`, `drop`, `sever`, `fuse τ`) reduces and **emits** its atomic
   grade (the nine families of §6 below). A trace's *observed retention*
   `ρ(run)` is the ▷-product of its emissions — by construction an element of
   the grade monoid.
2. *Grade soundness*, now stateable: if `Γ ⊢ e : A ! g` and `e ⇓ v` with trace
   `t`, then `g ⊑ ρ(t)`. The straight-line/β/let cases are the Agda Theorem B
   targets already proved; the subsumption and application cases need exactly
   L4 — which the amended carrier supplies. This is why the repair is not
   cosmetic: *without (A1)/(A2), conservative soundness is unprovable at the
   antichain; with them, the standard graded-monad argument goes through.*
3. *`fix` without synthesis.* The checker stays bidirectional: the author
   declares the latent grade γ; the checker verifies the *post-fixed-point
   condition* `F(γ) ⊒ γ` where `F` is the body's grade functional — decidable,
   since `F` is computable on grades and ⊑ is decidable (`decGradeLte`). KT
   (restored) guarantees `lfp F` exists and `γ ⊑ lfp F` for any such γ … the
   declared signature under-claims the true grade, which is the conservative
   direction. A terminating run of depth n has `ρ ⊒ Fⁿ(⊤̂)-grade ⊒ γ` by
   induction using L4. Unbounded lossy recursion still lands on ⊤ honestly.

Suggested split of #10: (a) ratify §3 repair; (b) mechanize the instrumented
semantics for the five atoms + β/let (extends Theorem B); (c) add `fix` with
the declared-signature rule and prove the depth-n induction. After (c), grade
soundness is a theorem, not a target.

== 6. O1 as a theorem (carrier-relative half)

`EffectAtoms.lean` proves, on the shipped carrier:

* An *atom* is a grade differing from ε in exactly one coordinate. Atoms fall
  into exactly **nine families** (`Fintype.card AtomFamily = 9`):
  attenuate/collapse/drop/falsify (fate), withhold/sever/misbind (bond),
  fuse/conflate (merge); the families partition by coordinate (4+3+2,
  `families_disjoint`), every family is inhabited (`family_realized`).
* **Atom factorization** (`atom_factorization`): every grade is the ▷-product
  of its six single-coordinate projections. The atoms generate the entire
  carrier — there is no move in the grade monoid that is not a composite of
  the nine families, and ε (`p-preserving`) is the empty product.

So the effect typology is *exhaustive relative to the carrier*. What remains
of O1 is only the metaphysical adequacy question (do the three coordinates
capture every way particularity can be lost?) — which is where it should be:
that is a claim about the world, not the algebra.

*Vocabulary seam made precise:* the nine families and the workbench's nine
effects do not align one-to-one. `p-preserving` names the empty product;
`p-projecting` covers two families (`drop` on a fate field, `withhold` on the
bond); the `withhold` family has no standalone effect name. A naming decision
for the workbench, not an algebra gap.

== 7. The cancellation classification (issue #14 closed, and sharpened)

`TierTwoComplete.lean` proves the two lemmas OPEN-C asked for
(`Bond.withheld_honest_not_cancel`, `Merge.fused_honest_not_cancel`) and then
the full classification:

* every non-unit element of every coordinate fails left-cancellation
  (`*.leftCancelWitness`) — including honest *finite* attenuation
  (`Fate.atten_not_cancel`: `atten δ` cannot separate `present` from
  `atten (Q 0)`);
* **`grade_leftCancel_iff`**: an element of the grade monoid is
  left-cancellative *iff it is ε*.

This sharpens Deliverable 3 of `GradeBoundary.lean`: the "tier-1 cancellative
core" `Q ℕ ≅ (ℕ,+,0)` lives inside the fidelity *dimension* and does not lift
to any non-unit grade element. At the grade level the tiers read: tier 1 =
`{ε}`; tier 2 = honest non-units; tier 3 = deceptive absorbers. Tier-2
membership is now exhaustive, closing OPEN-C.

*A fourth discrepancy, discovered en route (the `Attenuated(0)` quotient).*
v0.1 §3.1 declares `Attenuated(0) = Present`; the carrier keeps them distinct
values and imposes no normalization. The classification above is
carrier-level. Under the §3.1 identification, the collision witness
(`atten δ ▷ Present = atten δ ▷ Atten(Q 0)`) disappears, finite attenuation
becomes cancellative, and tier 1 at the grade level is exactly the original
"finite-fidelity + units" core. Both statements are right — about two
different carriers. The spec should either impose the quotient (normalize
`Atten (Q 0) → Present` in the carrier and the IR validator) or strike the
`Attenuated(0) = Present` line; this belongs with the #13 reconciliation.

== 8. Issue #13 — now four reconciliation items

Independent of the above, the v0.1 §3.1/§3.2 tables omit `Falsified` and
`Misbound`, and the §4 L3 line names `Dropped`/`Severed` (honest values) as
the absorbing bottoms where the proved carrier has `Falsified`/`Misbound`.
This note adds two more items to the same reconciliation: the
`Attenuated(0) = Present` quotient (§7 above), and — if (A1) is adopted — the
L3 line should say the deceptive bottoms absorb *two-sidedly*. A draft
reconciliation is proposed separately (owner call on whether to amend v0.1 in
place or via the v0.2 addendum).

*Note on (A2) and §3.1's "load-bearing incomparability".* §3.1 motivates
`Predicated ∥ Dropped` by: "a use-model needing only the predicate accepts
`Predicated(P)` but rejects `Dropped`." That behaviour is *preserved* by (A2):
the amendment adds `Dropped ⊑ Predicated`, not the converse, so a floor of
`Predicated` still rejects `Dropped`. The only behavioural change is that a
floor of `Dropped` (the weakest honest floor) now also accepts a checkbox —
and a retention floor that tolerates total withholding while rejecting
strictly-more-retentive states was never expressible in a monotone floor
semantics anyway. The documented intent survives; only the antichain goes.
