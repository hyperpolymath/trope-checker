== Verification topology (the trust boundary)

Beyond the RSR CI scaffold above, trope-checker carries a *proof-critical path*: the
calculus's meaning is machine-certified across three proof assistants by deliberate
role. A defect here is a defect in the soundness guarantee, so the layer is part of
the architecture, not a side artefact.

[cols="1,1,2", options="header"]
|===
| Layer | Language | Role

| `verification/proofs/lean4/grade-boundary/` | Lean 4 + Mathlib | *Grade-algebra ground truth* (authoritative): the honest/deceptive three-tier cancellation boundary. Sorry-free; `#print axioms` = `propext`/`Quot.sound` only.
| `verification/proofs/lean4/grade-factorisation/` | Lean 4 + Mathlib | *Tropical factorisation probe* (dynamics-free): imports the grade-boundary carrier and decides whether the grade admits a commutative cost-quotient. Verdict *(B) factors but veridicality-blind* — a nontrivial hom onto commutative `Δ` exists, but every commutative-target hom is forced to identify `dropped`/`falsified` (blindness co-located with the fate-axis non-commutativity). Precondition only — does *not* touch CO-1. Sorry-free; axiom-clean.
| `verification/proofs/agda/theorem-b/` | Agda (`--safe --without-K`) | *Calculus metatheory* (Theorem B): intrinsic graded calculus, value substitution, β/let subject reduction, Honest Generation. Zero postulates. (`fix`/lfp open — CO-1.)
| `verification/proofs/idris2/` | Idris2 | *Verified core* reused by the executable checker (`src/idris2/`) — the thing that runs is the thing that is proved.
| `tests/conformance/` | JSON + bash/jq | The portable-semantics anchor: any implementation must reproduce its IR→verdict pairs and witness edges.
|===

Gates: `just verify-all` (build Lean + Agda + honesty gate), `just build-lean`,
`just build-agda`. Rationale and the unified R-id ↔ Lean ↔ Agda ↔ status table:
`verification/proofs/INTEGRATION-REPORT.adoc`; decision record: ADR 0003.
