== Verdict (stated up front)

*(B) FACTORS but VERIDICALITY-BLIND.*

IMPORTANT: *Provenance (R-2026-07-07, ADR 0004).* The verdict above, and
everything down to the re-run section at the foot of this report, was proved
against the *pre-ratification* carrier (non-commutative fate coordinate, with
`Dropped ▷ Falsified = Dropped`). It remains a correct theorem about that
carrier. Under the *ratified* carrier the answer changes: see
<<rerun-ratified,Re-run under the ratified carrier (R-2026-07-07)>> — verdict
*(B′) FACTORS AND VERIDICALITY-SIGHTED*.

A nontrivial monoid homomorphism `π : (Grade, ▷, ε) → (Δ, +, Q 0)` onto the
commutative tropical fidelity carrier `Δ = WithTop (WithTop ℕ)` *exists* and is *proved
green* (`pi_hom`, `pi_one`, the bundled `piHom : Grade →* Multiplicative Δ`,
`pi_nontrivial`). But it *necessarily* identifies the honest withholding constructor
`dropped` with the deceptive constructor `falsified`. This collapse is not an artefact
of one cost-assignment: *every* multiplicative map from `Fate` into *any* commutative
monoid is forced to identify them (`forced_collapse_general`, `forced_collapse`,
`no_veridicality_faithful_hom`). Therefore the best case (A) — a homomorphism that also
preserves the honest/deceptive distinction — is *impossible*; and the dead case (C) — no
nontrivial homomorphism at all — is *false*.

Consequence, in the brief's own terms: *tropical captures the FIDELITY sub-grade only;
the fate-axis honest/deceptive distinction (`dropped` vs `falsified`) needs separate,
non-tropical handling; the route is partially viable.* The loss is precisely co-located
with the non-commutativity: the commutative coordinates (`Bond`, `Merge`) carry no such
obstruction and their deceptions remain tropically separable
(`psiBond_hom`, `psiBond_separates_honest_deceptive`). This is *not* "recursion solved"
— see the guard immediately below.

== Necessary-not-sufficient guard (verbatim, non-negotiable)

____
A POSITIVE result here means ONLY "the commutative cost-quotient EXISTS — the tropical
route is NOT EXCLUDED, precondition met." It does NOT mean "tropical solves recursion."
Whether recursion's grade actually lives in the commutative part needs an operational
model (CO-1) and stays parked. Any report wording that implies the fixpoint is solved,
handled, or even addressed is a FALSE CLAIM and must be removed. This probe can only
kill the tropical route or fail to kill it. It cannot confirm it.
____

The result here is *(B)*, i.e. the route is *not killed* but is *narrowed*: it survives
only for fidelity, and only as a precondition. Nothing in this subproject touches an
operational model, fix dynamics, the limit carrier, admissibility, or Scott-continuity.
Those are downstream of this probe and remain parked (see *Fireline*).

== Ground truth (STEP 0 — read from the source, nothing invented)

Quoted from `grade-boundary/GradeBoundary.lean`:

* *Fidelity carrier* (lines 75–105). `Δ = WithTop (WithTop ℕ)`; `dplus = (+)` is
  *commutative* (the underlying additive monoid is); `Q n = ↑↑n`, `total = ↑⊤` (the
  honest infinite loss, inner top), `unknown = ⊤` (the deceptive bottom of fidelity,
  outer top), unit `Q 0 = 0`. The absorption order is `unknown ⊐ total ⊐ finite`, with
  the *decisive asymmetry*
+
[source]
----
theorem total_add_unknown : total + unknown = unknown   -- GradeBoundary.lean:104
theorem unknown_absorbing (d) : unknown + d = unknown    -- left zero (L5)
theorem total_add_Q (n)      : total + Q n = total        -- total absorbs finite only
----

* *Fate composition* `comp`, first-match, clause-for-clause (lines 126–139). The two
  load-bearing facts:
+
[source]
----
| falsified, _ => falsified      -- falsified is left-absorbing
| dropped,   _ => dropped        -- dropped is ALSO left-absorbing
...                              -- so the two heads DISAGREE on order:
--   dropped  ▷ falsified = dropped     (dropped wins by first-match)
--   falsified ▷ dropped  = falsified   (falsified wins by first-match)
----
+
`Fate.not_comm` (GradeBoundary.lean:180) witnesses non-commutativity at exactly this
pair; `Δ` commutativity and this disagreement are the entire engine of the verdict.

== The claim under test

A candidate projection `π : (Grade, ▷, unit) → (Δ, +, Q 0)`, "extract accumulated
fidelity cost". `Δ` is the commutative tropical target. The question: is `π` a
*nontrivial monoid homomorphism*, and exactly what does it preserve and collapse?

== Deliverable 1 — the cost extraction `π`

Fidelity (a `Δ`) is nested only under `Fate.atten`; `Bond` and `Merge` carry no `Δ`.
So a fidelity cost factors through a per-slot map `φ : Fate → Δ`, summed over the four
fate slots, with `Bond`/`Merge` sent to the additive unit `Q 0` (equivalently, the
constant-`0` homomorphism). `pi g := φ g.fQuality + φ g.fBearer + φ g.fContext + φ
g.fRecord`.

*Modelling choices (flagged — these are choices, not facts).* The honest core is forced
in spirit but the boundary values are a modelling decision, and that decision is the
first place the probe can be gamed:

[cols="1,1,3",options="header"]
|===
| Constructor | Assigned `Δ`-cost | Status
| `present`    | `Q 0` (= 0)        | Obvious: no loss.
| `atten d`    | `d`                | *Choice* (the natural one): read off the stored
                                       fidelity payload. Any additive endo of `Δ` would
                                       respect composition; identity is the faithful read.
| `predicated` | `unknown`          | *Choice.* Forced to a top by `predicated ▷ atten d
                                       = predicated`; forced to `unknown` (not `total`)
                                       by the `atten unknown` case.
| `dropped`    | `unknown`          | *Choice, then forced by D4* to equal `falsified`.
| `falsified`  | `unknown`          | *Choice, then forced by D4* to equal `dropped`.
|===

The brief's natural first guess keeps honest and deceptive apart — `dropped ↦ total`,
`falsified ↦ unknown` — call it `phiV`. We carry it explicitly precisely so we can
falsify it.

== Deliverable 2 — the homomorphism test (falsifier-first)

*Positive half (the blind map factors).* `phi_hom : φ (a * b) = φ a + φ b` is proved by
an *exhaustive* case-split over all `5 × 5` `Fate` constructor pairings (the `atten d`
cases generic in `d`). Lifted over the four independent slots, `pi_hom : π (a ▷ b) = π a
+ π b` and `pi_one : π ε = Q 0` hold; `Δ`'s commutativity is used exactly once, to
rearrange the eight summands (`abel`). The homomorphism is packaged as a typeclass-level
object `piHom : Grade →* Multiplicative Δ`.

*Falsifier half (the faithful candidate breaks).* `piV_not_hom` is a *verified theorem*
that the veridicality-faithful `phiV` is *not* a homomorphism. The witness is the F2
pair `a = gDropped`, `b = gFalsified` (each unit except its quality slot):

[source]
----
phiV(dropped ▷ falsified) = phiV(dropped)            = total
phiV(dropped) + phiV(falsified) = total + unknown    = unknown
total ≠ unknown                                       -- (total_ne_unknown)
----

The absorbing-meet selects the constructor `dropped` by *first-match position* (cost
`total`); the commutative cost-sum selects `unknown` (the absorption-maximal top). They
disagree. The failure is *one-sided* — on `falsified ▷ dropped` it accidentally holds
(`unknown + total = unknown`) — which is the fingerprint of cost *entangled* with
non-commutative constructor identity. This is precisely the breaker the brief
anticipated.

== Deliverable 3 — nontriviality

`pi_nontrivial : π (gAtten (Q 1)) ≠ π (gAtten (Q 2))` — the homomorphism distinguishes
attenuation levels, so it is not the trivial collapse-to-unit. Guards against a vacuous
YES; see D6b for the adversary that makes this guard bite. Two reinforcements:
`pi_surjective` proves `π` is *onto* `Δ` (`π (gAtten d) = d`), so "onto" is literal; and
`pi_discrete_nontrivial : π ε ≠ π gDropped` shows nontriviality does not rest solely on
the `atten` coercion — `π` already separates grades on the discrete part.

== Deliverable 4 — veridicality discrimination, and why blindness is FORCED

This is the keystone, and it sharpens the verdict from "our chosen `π` happens to be
blind" to "no homomorphism can be otherwise".

`forced_collapse_general` (depends on *no axioms at all*): for *any* commutative monoid
`M` and *any* multiplicative `f : Fate → M`, `f dropped = f falsified`. Three lines:
`dropped ▷ falsified = dropped` gives `f dropped = f dropped + f falsified`;
`falsified ▷ dropped = falsified` gives `f falsified = f falsified + f dropped`;
commutativity of `M` equates the right-hand sides, hence the left.

Consequences:

* `no_veridicality_faithful_hom` — there is *no* multiplicative `Fate → Δ` keeping
  `dropped` apart from `falsified`. Outcome *(A) is impossible*, and not merely for the
  chosen `Δ` — for *any* commutative cost-carrier whatsoever.
* `pi_collapses_honest_deceptive : π gDropped = π gFalsified` — at grade level, `π`
  cannot tell honest withholding from deception.

Interpretation. `dropped` is honest withholding; `falsified` is a lie. A commutative
cost cannot keep them apart, because the carrier records *which lie/withholding wins a
composition by order*, and order is exactly what a commutative target discards. So the
tropical cost faithfully tracks *how much fidelity was lost* but is structurally blind
to *whether the fate-axis loss was honest or deceptive*.

*Scope of the loss (a precision the impossibility itself demands).* The obstruction is a
property of `Fate` specifically — the one coordinate with *two* disagreeing absorbing
heads (`dropped`, `falsified`). It is *not* a statement about veridicality wholesale.
`Bond` and `Merge` are *commutative* monoids (proved in `grade-boundary`), so their
deceptive bottoms (`misbound`, `conflated`) carry no F2-style obstruction and *are*
tropically separable. We mechanise this counterpoint: `psiBond_hom` is a genuine
(unital) homomorphism `Bond → Δ`, and `psiBond_separates_honest_deceptive` proves it
keeps the deceptive `misbound` apart from the honest `severed` (`total ≠ unknown`). Thus
`π` drops bond/merge veridicality only as a *modelling choice* (it ignores those
coordinates), whereas the *fate* honest/deceptive distinction is collapsed of
*necessity*. The veridicality content that is genuinely non-tropical is exactly the
fate-axis one; it lives in the non-commutative complement and needs separate handling.

Mechanised, fold-agnostic form. `forced_collapse_grade` (depends on *no axioms*) lifts
the obstruction to the whole grade: *any* multiplicative `f : Grade → M` into *any*
commutative monoid `M` has `f gDropped = f gFalsified`, because `gDropped` and
`gFalsified` are themselves a non-commuting pair (`gDropped ▷ gFalsified = gDropped`,
`gFalsified ▷ gDropped = gFalsified`). So no choice of how to fold the coordinates — sum
or otherwise, into `Δ` or any other commutative target — recovers the fate distinction.
(This obstruction is against *strict*, total, *commutative-target* homomorphisms; relaxing
strictness, totality, or commutativity of the target is exactly what "separate,
non-tropical handling" would mean.)

== Deliverable 5 — the complement: F2 confined

`pi_blind_to_noncomm : π (a ▷ b) = π (b ▷ a)` for *all* `a, b` — immediate from `pi_hom`
and commutativity of `Δ`. So `π` cannot see *any* non-commutativity; the carrier's F2
non-commutativity is confined entirely to the complement of `π` (it never reaches the
cost-quotient). The confinement is non-vacuous: `grade_F2_noncomm` proves the witness is
genuinely non-commutative (`gDropped ▷ gFalsified ≠ gFalsified ▷ gDropped`), while
`pi_collapses_F2` proves `π` identifies the two composites. The split is clean:
*commutative fidelity cost-quotient* `×` *non-commutative veridicality complement*.

== Deliverable 6 — falsifier harness (`just falsify`)

* *6a* — `piV_not_hom` (and its fate-level `phiV_not_hom`) is the live failing example:
  a verified theorem that the faithful candidate is not a homomorphism. `just falsify`
  asserts both are present in the axiom audit.
* *6b* — `phi0_hom` proves the trivial map (everything ↦ `Q 0`) *is* a homomorphism,
  while `phi0_not_nontrivial` proves it collapses attenuation levels. Together they show
  D3 does real work: a YES obtained only by collapsing everything is caught by
  nontriviality.
* *6c* — no sampling: `phi_hom` is by `cases a <;> cases b`, discharging every one of the
  `5 × 5` pairings (the `atten` cases generic), and `phi_hom_heads` re-exposes the pure
  absorbing-head subgrid explicitly.

== Fireline

This probe establishes a *precondition only*. It shows the commutative cost-quotient
exists (so the tropical route is not excluded for fidelity) and that veridicality cannot
ride in that quotient (so the tropical route is, at best, partial). Recursion-factoring
proper — whether the grade of a fixpoint actually lives in the commutative part —
remains parked behind *CO-1 (an operational model)*, together with O4 (the limit
carrier), admissibility, Scott-continuity, and `fix`/`lfp`. None of those is touched,
addressed, or implied here. The fixpoint is *not* solved, handled, or addressed by this
work.

=== Verdict

*(B′) FACTORS AND VERIDICALITY-SIGHTED.*

A nontrivial monoid homomorphism
`πR : (GradeR, ▷, ε) → (Δ, +, Q 0)` exists (`piR_hom`, `piR_one`, bundled
`piRHom : GradeR →* Multiplicative Δ`, `piR_nontrivial`, `piR_surjective`) *and
it distinguishes the honest `Dropped` from the deceptive `Falsified`*
(`phiR_sighted`, `piR_sighted`):

[cols="1,1,2",options="header"]
|===
| Constructor | Cost | Note
| `Present`    | `Q 0` (= 0)  | forced by the unit law
| `Atten d`    | `squash d`   | payload read-off with the deceptive top squashed: `⊤ ↦ ∞`, identity elsewhere (see the forced trade-off below)
| `Predicated` | `∞` (`total`) | honest collapse
| `Dropped`    | `∞` (`total`) | honest withholding — *kept apart from deception*
| `Falsified`  | `⊤` (`unknown`) | the deceptive top
|===

`Dropped ↦ ∞` vs `Falsified ↦ ⊤`, and `∞ ≠ ⊤` (`total_ne_unknown`). What still
collapses: `Predicated`/`Dropped`/honest-total-loss all land on `∞`
(`phiR_collapses_predicated_dropped`) — the hom is blind to *which honest*
absorber acted, no longer to *whether* the absorber was honest.

*Conclusion: the veridicality-blindness of the historical verdict (B) was an
artifact of the pre-ratification carrier.* The forcing argument
(`forced_collapse_general`) ran through the non-commuting pair
`dropped ▷ falsified = dropped` / `falsified ▷ dropped = falsified`; (A1)
replaced the first clause with `dropped ▷ falsified = falsified`, the pair now
commutes (the whole ratified algebra does — `GradeR` is a `CommMonoid`), and
the separation `π(D) + π(F) = π(F)` is satisfiable with `∞ + ⊤ = ⊤`.

=== Honest reporting: where the brief's naive candidate breaks, and the forced repair

The issue-#29 candidate reads the attenuation payload verbatim
(`atten d ↦ d`). That map is *not* a homomorphism on the ratified carrier:
`phiId_not_hom` is a verified breaker at `atten ⊤ ▷ dropped = dropped`, where
the law would need `∞ = ⊤ + ∞ = ⊤`. The repair — squash the deceptive payload
top inside attenuation (`squash : ⊤ ↦ ∞`, identity elsewhere; an additive
monoid endomorphism of `Δ`, `squash_add`/`squash_zero`) — is not ad hoc:
`sighted_forces_squash` proves *every* multiplicative `f : Fate → Δ` on the
ratified composition with `f dropped ≠ f falsified` must have
`f (atten ⊤) ≠ ⊤`. So fate-axis sightedness necessarily costs payload-top
sightedness (`atten ∞` and `atten ⊤` become indistinguishable to the cost).
The two veridicality axes — fate-level and payload-level — cannot both ride in
one commutative coordinate; post-ratification, either one *can*, which is
exactly what was impossible for the fate axis before.
