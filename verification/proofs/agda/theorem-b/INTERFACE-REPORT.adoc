== What this is

Theorem B is the deferred metatheory of the trope-particularity *calculus* (spec
`spec/calculus.adoc`, `:revnumber: 0.1`) over the grade algebra. It is developed
*intrinsically* in Agda 2.8.0 + agda-stdlib, `--safe --without-K`, in
`verification/proofs/agda/theorem-b/`, REUSING the echo-types graded-reindexing
*style* (`echo-types/proofs/agda/EchoGraded.agda` вЂ” the thin-poset reindexing
modality; `EchoIndexed.agda` вЂ” indexed functoriality; these are the
"`EchoVariance`" graded muscle the task names).

It sits ON TOP of the *FROZEN INTERFACE* вЂ” the grade algebra established in Lean 4
(`verification/proofs/lean4/grade-boundary/GradeBoundary.lean`, sorry-free, axioms
`propext`/`Quot.sound` only). The four established facts are re-established in Agda
here as the foundation (`TB.Grade`), then the calculus metatheory is built over them.

=== Target 1 вЂ” encode the grade as a NON-COMMUTATIVE graded structure (intrinsic) вЂ” *PROVED*

=== Target 2 вЂ” substitution-admissibility / reindexing ("tropes follow values through cut") вЂ” *PROVED + sharpness refutation*

The substitution lemma `subst : Subst О“ О” в†’ О“ вЉў A ! g в†’ О” вЉў A ! g` preserves the
grade `g` *EXACTLY* вЂ” the conclusion's grade is the same `g`, no в–· reorder anywhere.
This is the machine-checked form of "tropes follow values through cut". It is
insensitive to F2 precisely because a value (and a variable) carries Оµ, the
two-sided unit of в–·; single substitution `_[_]` inherits exactness.

The F2 boundary is itself a *theorem*, not a gap: `cut-left-grade` pins the oriented

value lemma is *sharp* вЂ” F2 forbids its symmetric generalisation. This is exactly
the predicted F2 wall, delivered as a refutation rather than an unproven obligation.

[IMPORTANT]
.FINDING (F2Г—F4): tier-preservation is CLOSURE, not a homomorphism
====
The naive single-tier meet-homomorphism `tierOf (g в–· h) в‰Ў tierOf g вЉ“ tierOf h` is
*FALSE* on the fate coordinate вЂ” `fate-tier-meet-hom-fails`: `dropped в–·f falsified
= dropped` (honest), but `fateTier dropped вЉ“ fateTier falsified = deceptive`. The
honest left-absorbing head `dropped` *shields* a later deceptive `falsified`. This
is the same non-commutativity phenomenon (F2) as the L4-monotonicity failure
(`fate-L4-fails`, ported from `Soundness.idr`). It is *why* tier-preservation had to
be stated as set-membership closure and *why* the вЉ‘/L4 route is unsound вЂ” both are
kept as compiled guard theorems so no later work re-introduces the wrong route.
