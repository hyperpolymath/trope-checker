= ADR 0004 — Ratified carrier amendments R-2026-07-07: (A1) two-sided deceptive zeros, (A2) chain fate order, (A3) Attenuated(0) normalization
:revdate: 2026-07-07

== Status

Accepted — ratified by the owner 2026-07-07 (blanket ratification of the PR #26
decision checklist). Carrier migration in the same PR as this ADR.

== Context

The L4 monotonicity analysis (`spec/notes/2026-07-07-l4-boundary-and-atoms.adoc`,
machine-verified in `verification/proofs/lean4/grade-boundary/L4Monotonicity.lean`,
landed via PR #25) localised every failure of spec v0.1's L4 to two design
points in the fate coordinate: the `{Predicated, Dropped}` antichain, and the
clause `Dropped ▷ Falsified = Dropped`. That clause was simultaneously the sole
source of the grade algebra's non-commutativity and an L5 laundering hole (an
upstream honest drop erased a downstream lie). With L4 broken, §7's
Knaster–Tarski justification for `fix`'s least-fixed-point grade was unsound as
written, and conservative grade-soundness reasoning was unprovable at the
antichain — the central blocker behind issues #10 (CO-1) and #12 (O4).
Separately, the #14 work discovered that §3.1's `Attenuated(0) = Present`
identification is declared by the spec but imposed nowhere in the carrier.

== Decisions

[cols="1,3",options="header"]
|===
| ID | Decision

| *R-2026-07-07-01 (A1)* | `Dropped ▷ Falsified = Falsified`. Each deceptive
bottom (`Falsified`, `Misbound`, `Conflated`, fidelity's `Unknown`) is a
*two-sided* zero of its coordinate's composition. L5 strengthens: no honest
absorber launders deception, in either composition order.

| *R-2026-07-07-02 (A2)* | `Dropped ⊑ Predicated` in the fate retention order.
The fate order becomes the chain `Falsified ⊏ Dropped ⊏ Predicated ⊏
Attenuated(⊤) ⊏ Attenuated(∞) ⊏ … ⊏ Attenuated(0) ⊏ Present`. The §3.1
"duck check" behaviour that mattered is preserved: a floor of `Predicated(P)`
still rejects `Dropped` (the converse direction was added, not that one), and
`Predicated` remains strictly below every attenuation.

| *R-2026-07-07-03 (A3)* | The `Attenuated(0) = Present` identification of
§3.1 is *imposed by normalization at IR ingest*: validators rewrite
`Attenuated(Q 0)` to `Present` before grading. Composition preserves normal
forms (finite tropical addition reaches 0 only from 0+0), so the algebra's
carrier datatypes stay as-is and stored/composed grades are normal forms.
Checker-side implementation tracked in a follow-up issue.
|===

== Consequences

* **L4 holds in full** (both arguments) on the amended carrier; fate
  composition is **commutative**; every coordinate is a **complete chain**.
  §7's Knaster–Tarski argument is restored, unblocking the CO-1 operational
  model (#10) and settling O4 (#12: the `ℕ ∪ {∞,⊤}` carrier suffices —
  transfinite carriers would require transfinitely deep `fix` nesting, which
  finitary syntax cannot express).
* **The Idris2 verified core and Rust fast-core are migrated** in this PR;
  `Trope.Soundness.fateL4MonotonicityFails` (a theorem only of the
  pre-ratification carrier) is replaced by total monotonicity proofs in both
  arguments.
* **Historical proofs retain their meaning under a provenance note.**
  `GradeBoundary.lean`'s `grade_mul_not_comm` and the `grade-factorisation`
  probe's "veridicality-blind" verdict are theorems about the
  *pre-ratification* carrier (non-commutativity turned out to be an artifact
  of the unamended clause, not intrinsic to loss-shape grading). Their files
  carry header notes; a full Lean re-mirror of the ratified carrier and a
  re-run of the factorisation question are follow-ups.
* **Conformance expectations may legitimately change** only where a verdict
  compares `Dropped` against `Predicated` across a floor (A2) — any such case
  is updated with an inline justification in the same PR.
* The Agda Theorem B development embeds its own carrier mirror and needs the
  same migration before its results are cited against the ratified carrier —
  follow-up issue.

== Rejected alternatives

* *Keep the antichain, restrict `fix` to a monotone fragment* — rejected:
  leaves conservative soundness unprovable exactly where collapse meets
  possible-drop, which real pipelines hit constantly.
* *Repair by order surgery alone (A2 without A1)* — rejected: the laundering
  clause still breaks left-argument monotonicity toward `Falsified` and keeps
  the algebra non-commutative for no semantic gain.
* *Strike `Attenuated(0) = Present` instead of imposing it* — rejected: two
  representations of "no loss" complicate cancellation structure and floor
  semantics for no benefit; normalization at ingest is one line per validator.
