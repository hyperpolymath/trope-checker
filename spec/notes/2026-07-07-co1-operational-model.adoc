= Design note: the CO-1 operational model — instrumented semantics, tagged values, and the declared-signature fix rule
:revdate: 2026-07-07
:toc: macro

[.lead]
The design that closes CO-1 (issue #10): a small-step semantics for a core
trope-particularity term language in which *grade soundness is stateable and
provable*. Builds on the ratified carrier (ADR 0004 — L4 now holds in full,
proved in the Idris2 ground truth as `fateL4MonoR`/`fateL4MonoL`). This note
is the design for review; the mechanization plan (§6) extends the Agda
Theorem B development once its carrier mirror is migrated (issue #30).

toc::[]

== 1. The core language λ-trope

Values:

* provenance-enriched tropes stem:[t = \langle q, b, c, r\rangle] (quality
  payload, bearer, context, record);
* collapse residues (the predicate's codomain, e.g. booleans), floating
  qualities (post-`sever`), fused tropes (carrying the provenance tag τ);
* functions.

Terms: variables, λ-abstraction, application, `let`, `fix`, and the five
atomic moves as primitive operators — `attenuate_δ e`, `predicate_P e`,
`drop_φ e`, `sever e`, `fuse_τ e₁ e₂` — with `preserve` the identity and the
surface effects (`project`, `collapse`, `detach`) elaborated to composites, as
in the calculus. The deceptive moves are not term formers (prevent profile).

== 2. Instrumented semantics: tagged values, not traces

Each trope-carrying value runs *tagged*: stem:[v@h] where stem:[h] is the
grade of everything that has happened to this particular since it entered the
frame. Tags are ghost state — they never influence control flow (§6, O-CO1-b).

Small-step rules (β, `let`, congruence) carry tags unchanged and emit ε.
Each atomic move composes its atomic grade onto the tag of its operand:

  attenuate_δ (t@h)  ↝  t'@(h ▷ a_δ)          -- a_δ = the attenuate atom
  predicate_P (t@h)  ↝  p@(h ▷ a_P)           -- collapse residue
  drop_φ (t@h)       ↝  t'@(h ▷ a_{drop φ})
  sever (t@h)        ↝  fq@(h ▷ a_sever)      -- FloatingQuality
  fuse_τ (t₁@h₁) (t₂@h₂) ↝ t'@((h₁ ⊓ h₂) ▷ a_{fuse τ})

The *observed retention* of a run is read off the output value's tag — no
separate trace bookkeeping. For `fuse`, the meet of the constituent tags is
the worst case across lineages, matching the checker's treatment of a
multi-parent node (exact IR-DAG correspondence is O-CO1-c, the bridge to O2).
The atoms are exactly the nine families of `EffectAtoms.lean` (honest fragment:
the five moves; ε = empty product).

== 3. The soundness theorem, now stateable

[stem]
++++
\vdash e : A \,!\, g
\quad\wedge\quad
e \Downarrow v@h
\quad\Longrightarrow\quad
g \sqsubseteq h .
++++

The declared/derived grade never over-claims retention against any
terminating run. This is the theorem the spec calls "grade soundness" and
could not previously state for lack of a model of stem:[\rho]; here
stem:[\rho(\text{run}) = h].

Why the ratified carrier is what makes the proof go through: T-Sub and the
application case compose an *assumed* grade against an *actual* one, and
concluding stem:[\sqsubseteq]-preservation there is precisely L4 — refuted on
the pre-ratification carrier, total-proved on the ratified one. Theorem B's
existing targets (substitution grade-exactness, β/let subject reduction)
cover the exact fragment; L4 extends them to the conservative fragment.

== 4. `fix` without computing fixed points

Let stem:[F(\gamma)] be the body's derived latent grade under the assumption
that the recursive binder has latent grade stem:[\gamma] — computable, and
*monotone in* stem:[\gamma] (O-CO1-a below, by induction on the typing
derivation using L4 at every composition site).

*The rule (T-Fix-Decl).* The author declares stem:[\gamma]; the checker
accepts iff

[stem]
++++
\gamma \;\sqsubseteq\; F(\gamma)
++++

— decidable: stem:[F] is computable on grades and stem:[\sqsubseteq] is
decidable (`decGradeLte`). No fixed point is ever computed.

*Soundness of the rule*, by induction on call depth of a terminating run:
a depth-stem:[n] call's body makes inner calls of depth stem:[<n], whose
actual retention is stem:[\sqsupseteq \gamma] by the induction hypothesis; by
monotonicity of stem:[F] and the main soundness IH for the body, the call's
actual retention is stem:[\sqsupseteq F(\gamma) \sqsupseteq \gamma]. A
call that never recurses gives stem:[F(\gamma)] independent of the
hypothesis, and the same chain closes. Unbounded lossy recursion still lands
honestly: for a body that attenuates and recurses, no finite-δ signature is a
post-fixed point, and the strongest accepted signatures sit at
`Attenuated(∞)`/`Attenuated(⊤)` — the spec's "⊤ is an honest answer,"
sharpened.

*Fixed-point bookkeeping (a clarification the spec needs).* §7 says the grade
is the *least fixed point* — that is least in the **loss** order. In the
retention order stem:[\sqsubseteq] used everywhere else, the semantic optimum
is the **greatest post-fixed point** (Knaster–Tarski, now valid: complete
chains + monotone stem:[F]), i.e. stem:[\mathrm{gfp}\,F = \bigsqcup\{\gamma :
\gamma \sqsubseteq F(\gamma)\}] — the *tightest declaration the rule can
certify*. Worked check: body = "return preserved or attenuate-by-1 and
recurse" has stem:[F(\gamma) = \varepsilon \sqcap (a_1 \triangleright
\gamma)]; the greatest post-fixed point is `Attenuated(∞)`, which is exactly
the stem:[\sqsubseteq]-infimum of the actual retentions
stem:[\{A(n) : n\in\mathbb{N}\}] over terminating runs. Recommend §7 be
restated in these terms (one sentence; no rule change).

Kleene iteration and ω-continuity are *not needed* anywhere — existence via
Knaster–Tarski, checking via the post-fixed-point test, soundness via depth
induction.

== 5. What this discharges, and what it leaves

Discharged (once mechanized): the CO-1 hole — an operational model with a
defined stem:[\rho]; grade soundness as a theorem over it, covering
subsumption, application, and `fix`. Left where they belong: O2 (each
front-end's lowering must map its constructs to these atoms faithfully —
O-CO1-c gives the IR-side target to lower *to*); O5 (veridicality remains
declared, not checked); O1's metaphysical-adequacy residue.

== 6. Mechanization plan (Agda, extending Theorem B; gated on #30)

1. *O-CO1-b, tag erasure*: erasing tags commutes with reduction — tags are
   ghost. (Small; do first, it validates the instrumentation.)
2. Atomic-move steps: emission equals the typing rule's grade exactly —
   extends Target 2's exactness style.
3. T-Sub + application via the migrated L4 lemmas (Agda mirrors of
   `fateL4MonoR/L`, per #30).
4. *O-CO1-a*: stem:[F] monotone in the assumed latent grade — induction over
   typing derivations, L4 at each ▷/⊓ site.
5. T-Fix-Decl soundness by the depth induction of §4 — this is the step
   Theorem B's Target 3 marked NOT-REACHED; the rule shape (side condition on
   a declared γ, no lfp computation) is what makes it reachable.
6. Executable side: `tropecheck` already checks declared signatures against
   composed path grades; the new obligation is only the T-Fix-Decl side
   condition γ ⊑ F(γ) at recursive boundaries of the *front-end* (Haec), not
   the IR checker — record in Haec's elaboration obligations (O2 table).

With 1–5 landed, `docs/status/PROOF-STATUS` can finally list *grade
soundness: PROVED (core fragment)* — the headline the PL paper needs.
