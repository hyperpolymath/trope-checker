= Trope-checker Roadmap

== Current status

Alpha. The schema, executable checker (Idris2), and conformance suite are green; the
verified core typechecks with no axioms behind its claimed lemmas. The grade-algebra
*honest/deceptive cancellation boundary* is proved in Lean (sorry-free, axiom-clean)
and its metatheory partially developed in Agda (Theorem B). Ground truth and true
statuses: `docs/status/PROOF-STATUS.adoc`, `AFFIRMATION.adoc`,
`verification/proofs/INTEGRATION-REPORT.adoc`.

== Open problems (named, not solved)

* *CO-1 (central):* the calculus has *no operational model* for `fix`/routing dynamics — there is
  no reduction rule for `fix` and no observed-retention `ρ`. Gated additionally by v0.1's *O4* (the
  limit carrier for ordering iterated attenuation beyond ω, the `Conat`-vs-tag fork). Resolving CO-1
  unblocks full subject reduction (Theorem B Target 3).
* Grade soundness for *conservative* (inexact) lowering over fate — needs the lax-L4 argument
  (strict L4 is machine-proved to fail on fate).
* *O1* exhaustiveness of the seven effects; *O2* per-front-end lowering correctness; *O3* the
  produce/demand adjunction.
* Documentation reconciliation: *OPEN-B/B′* (`Falsified`/`Misbound` absent from v0.1 §3.1/§3.2);
  *OPEN-C* (`Withheld`/`Fused` tier-2 membership not individually witnessed).
