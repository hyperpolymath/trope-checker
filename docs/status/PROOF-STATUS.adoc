=== FINDING: strict L4 monotonicity FAILS for the fate coordinate

`Trope.Soundness.fateL4MonotonicityFails` machine-checks a counterexample:
`Dropped Ôèæ Present` and `Predicated Ôèæ Predicated`, yet
`Dropped ÔûÀ Predicated = Dropped` while `Present ÔûÀ Predicated = Predicated`, and
`Dropped Ôïó Predicated` (Predicated and Dropped are *intentionally incomparable*,
calculus ┬º3.1). So strict L4 does not hold for fate; the calculus's "L4 is a *lax*
compatibility" (┬º5 note) is load-bearing. Consequence: grade soundness for
*conservative* (inexact) steps cannot be composed by naive L4 over the fate
coordinate; the *exact* route (`soundFromExact`) sidesteps it, and bond/merge remain
monotone.
